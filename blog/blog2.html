Top 10 EV Charging Stations in India: A Comprehensive Guide

Published on August 22, 2025 · By Admin

As electric vehicles (EVs) become more popular, the demand for reliable charging infrastructure is rising. To support the growing number of EV owners, numerous charging stations have been established across India. This article highlights the top 10 EV charging stations in India, providing a comprehensive guide for EV drivers.

1. Tata Power EZ Charge

Tata Power EZ Charge is one of the largest and most reliable networks of EV charging stations in India. With stations located in major cities and along highways, Tata Power ensures convenient access to charging facilities for EV owners.

2. Fortum Charge & Drive

Fortum Charge & Drive is a global leader in EV charging solutions. In India, Fortum has partnered with various stakeholders to set up fast-charging stations, particularly in metropolitan areas. Their network is known for its reliability and user-friendly interface.

3. Ather Grid

Ather Grid, established by Ather Energy, offers a robust network of charging points primarily focused on two-wheelers. Many of their stations are also compatible with four-wheelers and are strategically placed in high-traffic areas.

4. ChargeGrid by Magenta

ChargeGrid, powered by Magenta, provides slow and fast chargers for different EV models. Their mobile app gives real-time updates on charger availability and status, making charging easy for EV drivers.

5. Statiq

Statiq is rapidly expanding its network of charging stations across India. Their user-friendly app allows EV owners to locate and reserve charging slots easily, with stations often in malls, offices, and residential areas.

6. EESL EV Charging Network

The Energy Efficiency Services Limited (EESL) has established an extensive network of EV charging stations in government buildings and public places, promoting sustainable transportation.

7. Volttic EV Chargers

Volttic offers a growing network in North and West India, designed for both personal and commercial EVs, providing reliable and efficient charging solutions.

8. PlugNGo

PlugNGo is a new but fast-growing network with user-friendly stations focused on urban areas and major highways.

9. EV Motors (PlugNgo)

EV Motors India has set up a comprehensive network of fast-charging stations under the brand PlugNgo, with advanced features for a seamless charging experience.

10. Relux Electric

Relux Electric focuses on innovative and sustainable charging solutions, expanding across India to provide efficient charging for both personal and commercial EVs.

“With the increasing adoption of EVs, reliable charging infrastructure is key to a smooth and hassle-free driving experience.”

Conclusion

The top 10 EV charging stations listed above provide convenient and efficient charging solutions for EV owners across India. Stay updated with TelioEV for the latest information on EV charging networks and other EV-related news.
